Schokofreude sales performance

An interactive recreation of a Power BI dashboard built to track sales performance across team members and countries for the Schokofreude chocolate company. Click any row in the table to see how that sales person's category mix changes.

What this shows

This dashboard tracks sales performance for a team of six sales people ("Juicies") across six countries, broken down by three chocolate categories: Bars, Bites, and Other.

Key finding: across all countries, Bars consistently outsold the other categories by a wide margin. Among the team, Oby Sorrel had the strongest bar sales in Australia, while Madelene Upcott had a more even spread across all countries rather than one standout market.

Recommendations

1. Give each sales person the tools and support to grow further in the category and market where they already perform best, since that is where they have built relationships and momentum.

2. For categories with weaker sales (like "Other"), bundle those products with bestsellers so customers can try them and build demand over time.
